Cool Lime

Ingredients:

  • 2 lemons
  • 3 sprigs of mint
  • 4 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3.5 cups of cold water
  • Ice

Instructions:

  1. Grate the rind of two lemons into a bowl. Add mint leaves and sugar. We crush this mixture by squeezing it with our hands.
  2. We squeeze the juice of the two lemons that we grated the peel into this mixture. We crush it again with our hands. Then add cold water and mix. Filter it with the help of a sieve. A bottle of cool lime is ready :)
  3. While serving, we throw a few pieces of ice into the glass and serve it that way. You should definitely try this practical homemade recipe that will cool you down like buzz in cold weather, everyone who has already tried it, enjoy it.
