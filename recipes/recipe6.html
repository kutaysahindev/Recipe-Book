Fit Brownies

Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tea glass of honey
  • 1 teaspoon of milk
  • 2.5 teaspoons of cocoa
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 cups of flour
  • 50gr dark chocolate
  • 1 cup of hazelnuts

Instructions:

  1. Whisk the egg and honey until frothy. Then add baking powder, cocoa and milk into it and mix.
  2. Then add the flour and mix until it thickens. When we add hazelnut and chocolate and mix it homogeneously, we pour it into my oil paper-lined debt.
